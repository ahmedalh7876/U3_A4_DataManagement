Instructions:
- Enter the name of the two students. The other text fields are not editable.
- To fill the remaining text fields, use the external file and input the 1st mark
of student 1 in line 1, the 1st subject of student 1 in line 2, the 2nd mark of

spaces (characters produced by clicking spacebar) after the end of each line.
